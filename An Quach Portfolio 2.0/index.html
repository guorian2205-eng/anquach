An Quach (Rory)
— /ɑːn kwɑːk/, or just Rory

A business student at RMIT University Vietnam, majoring in Economics and Finance. My work has moved across market research, consumer behaviour, and strategic analysis, with growing interest in fintech, sustainability, and digital transformation in emerging markets.

I am not curious about everything. But when something feels meaningful, I want to understand it fully. Curiosity has been how I find beauty in ideas, systems, and overlooked details. It is also why I care about research. I value the part of research where a broad question becomes clear enough to say something true.
